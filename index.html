Why choosing a career feels confusing in India

Most students are shown only a few “safe” or socially accepted options. Rare but valid paths are ignored. Decisions are often driven by pressure, fear, or incomplete information.

Course outcomes, competition levels, effort required, and long-term realities are rarely explained honestly.

Career decisions should not be guesswork. They should be informed.

How MyCareerTree is different

Tree-based thinking
Careers are connected. One choice leads to many future paths. We show the full structure — not isolated options.

Complete transparency
Popular, unpopular, rare, risky — all valid paths are shown honestly.

Reality over hype
Every path includes effort, competition, and long-term reality — not fake motivation or guaranteed success.

Indian education first
Designed strictly around how the Indian education and examination system actually works.

How the Career Tree works

  1. Start from your current stage (usually Class 10)
  2. Choose a branch — Science, Commerce, Arts, Diploma, ITI, etc.
  3. Explore what each choice realistically leads to
  4. Move forward step by step, without blind backtracking

You never need to “go back” randomly. Every step is connected forward.
