Science After Class 10

Science is the most popular — and the most demanding — stream after Class 10 in India. It opens doors to engineering, medical, defence, research, and more, but it also comes with pressure and competition.

What Does Choosing Science Mean?

Students who choose Science in Classes 11–12 study Physics and Chemistry along with Mathematics and/or Biology.

This stream is suitable for students who are comfortable with concepts, problem-solving, and continuous academic effort.

Science Subject Combinations

Science is not a single path. It has multiple combinations:

  • PCM – Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • PCB – Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • PCMB – Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology

Reality Check (Must Read)

Science is not for everyone. Competition is high, entrance exams are tough, and success requires consistent effort.

Many students choose Science due to family pressure without understanding their interest or aptitude. This often leads to stress and dropouts.
