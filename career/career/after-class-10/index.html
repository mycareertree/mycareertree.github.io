Class 10 is the point where real choices begin in the Indian education system. Below are all valid paths you can take after Class 10 — not just the popular ones.

Why This Stage Matters

Until Class 10, students in India follow almost the same academic path. After that, multiple routes open up — academic, technical, vocational, and alternative.

Choosing without understanding all options often leads to regret later. MyCareerTree shows every possible route honestly so you can decide wisely.
